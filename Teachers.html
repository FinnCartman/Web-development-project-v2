Teachers

Mr Nathan

Mr Nathan is the Senior Deputy Principal at Wakatipu High School. He is also a math teacher that teaches a few classes every year. He is an awesome teacher to talk about your issues with the school with as he is in a postion of power where listening to students opinion is useful to him. He is also a keen basketball entusiast coaching the senior boys 1st V basketball team.

Miss Kelly

Miss Kelly is a History and social studies teacher. She is also the Head Of Learning Area for social sciences. She is a very keen teacher who is well versed on history among other skills learnt throughout her life. She enjoys helping students with their schoolwork as well as being good with a joke.

Miss Panapa

Miss Panapa a Deputy Principal at the school. She is also a English teacher. She is very interested in sports and even coaches the volleyball team. If you are intersted in playing volleyball you will get to know Ms Panapa very well. She is also an excellent English teacher and if you get her for one of your classes consider yourself lucky.

Miss Scott

Miss Scott is a well liked Health and P.E teacher. She is also the Dean of Mackenzie house. She is very good to approach about sports and what ones are the best for you. Also if you are in Mackenzie house you will be seeing her a lot. She is very passionate about house events so it is best to get involved to be on her good side.

Mr Richards

Mr Richards is a Math Teacher as well as being the NCEA principals nominee. If you are a member of the junior school you will not have much interaction with him, but once you hit year 11 he will become a very important teacher for you to know. He is in charge of all extensions and exams on NCEA subjects so you will be seeing him a lot once you hit the senior school.

Ms Hiestand

Miss Hiestand is a Math teacher and assistant principal. She is a great and caring math teacher. She is a member of the Mackenzie house and gets involved in all of the house events. She is very helpful as a teacher and great for a good chat.
