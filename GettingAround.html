Getting Around

Te KiriKiri

Te KiriKiri is the social space in the centre of the school where we meet for important events. It also houses some basketball courts and nice outdoor seating with shade sails to sit underneath.

Stadium

The Stadium is where a large amount of sports are played basketball, dodgeball and volleyball are just a few. It is where a house basketball takes place also and is open at breaks for students to use.

Fields

The Fields are a large area out front of the High School. They are used a breaktimes by amny students for playing sports and hanging out. They are alos our home Rugby and Football grounds where our teams play there home games so if you are here on a weekend in winter you might want to check them out.
